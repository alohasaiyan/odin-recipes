Oyakodon

Description

Oyakodon is a Japanese rice bowl dish, in which chicken, egg, sliced scallion (or sometimes regular onions), and other ingredients are all simmered together in a kind of soup that is made with soy sauce and stock, and then served on top of a large bowl of rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked white rice
  • 4 oz chicken thigh meat, diced
  • 1 oz sliced scallion
  • 2 oz sliced onion
  • 1/2 cup light soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 beaten egg

Steps

  1. Put chicken, onions, soy sauce and sugar together in a small frying pan. Cover and set at medium heat for 10 minutes approximately
  2. After 10 minutes, check chicken for doneness. If it's cooked, add beaten egg
  3. When egg starts to coagulate, pour all contents of the pan over hot rice in a bowl
  4. Add fresh scallions and sesame oil over the bowl. Enjoy!
